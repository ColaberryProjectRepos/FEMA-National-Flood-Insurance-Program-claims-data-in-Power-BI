Problem Statement 1

The United States coastline is approximately 12,383 miles long and some regions are historically prone to flooding due to Hurricanes and Severe Storms.  Residents along the Gulf Coast and Eastern Coast of the US are frequently impacted, sometimes resulting in a National Disaster.  National Disaster weather events occur every 2 years on average.  When National Disaster weather events occur, the average number of claims rise from 32K to 127K and  costs rise from $35K to $66K per claim.
